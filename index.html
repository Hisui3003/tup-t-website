VISION

TUP: A premier state university with recognized excellence in engineering and technology education at par with leading universities in the ASEAN region.

MISSION

The mission of TUP is stated in Section 2 of P.D. No. 1518 as follows:

The University shall provide higher and advanced vocational, technical, industrial, technological and professional education and training in industries and technology, and in practical arts leading to certificates, diplomas and degrees. It shall provide progressive leadership in applied research, developmental studies in technical, industrial, and technological fields and production using indigenous materials; effect technology transfer in the countryside; and assist in the development of small-and-medium scale industries in identified growth centers.

CORE VALUES

T - Transparent and participatory governance.
U - Unity in the pursuit of TUP mission, goals, and objectives.
P - Professionalism in the discharge of quality service.
I - Integrity and commitment to maintain the good name of the University.
A - Accountability for individual and organizational quality performance.
N - Nationalism through tangible contribution to the rapid economic growth of the country.
S- Shared responsibility, hard work, and resourcefulness in compliance to the mandates of the university.

Excellence in Education

Since its establishment in 1977, TUP Taguig has been dedicated to providing quality education in engineering and technology. It began as a technical and vocational institution and has since evolved to offer advanced diploma and degree programs.

With its mission to produce industry-ready graduates, TUPT continues to stand as a pillar of technological education in the region.

Innovative Learning

TUP Taguig embraces modernization by integrating virtual learning platforms and updated facilities. These efforts enhance the academic experience, ensuring that students are equipped with the skills to thrive in modern industries.

The university continues to adapt its methods to meet global standards, fostering a dynamic learning environment for its students.

Hands-on Collaboration

TUP Taguig is committed to maintaining a hands-on approach to learning through real-world applications and industry collaborations. It encourages innovation, teamwork, and practical problem-solving, preparing students for professional success.

Through internships and community engagements, the university ensures its graduates are well-rounded and industry-ready.

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE)

Study the principles of civil engineering, focusing on designing and constructing infrastructure like roads, bridges, and buildings.

Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BSEE)

Learn about electrical systems, including circuits, power generation, and electrical equipment used in various industries.

Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering (BSECE)

Focus on electronics and communication systems, working with devices such as semiconductors, circuits, and mobile technology.

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME)

Study mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science, with applications in designing machines, tools, and mechanical systems.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Learn to develop and manage software systems, including database management, web development, and network administration.

Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science (BSESSDP)

Focus on environmental issues such as climate change, sustainability, and conservation, while learning the science behind environmental problems.

Bachelor of Technical Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) - Electrical Technology

Prepare to teach electrical technology to students, covering the fundamentals of electrical systems, wiring, and maintenance.

Bachelor of Technical Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) - Electronics Technology

Specialize in teaching electronics technology, including circuit design, repair, and electronics safety standards.

Bachelor of Technical Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) - Computer Programming

Learn to teach computer programming, including software development, coding languages, and application development techniques.

Bachelor of Technical Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) - Computer Hardware Servicing

Prepare to teach computer hardware servicing, including troubleshooting, repairs, and assembly of computer systems.

Bachelor of Engineering Technology (BET) - Automotive Technology

Focus on vehicle maintenance, diagnostics, and automotive systems, including engines, brakes, and electrical systems.

Bachelor of Engineering Technology (BET) - Chemical Technology

Study the principles of chemical processes used in manufacturing and product development, such as refining and polymers.

Bachelor of Engineering Technology (BET) - Civil Technology

Learn about civil construction, focusing on roads, bridges, and other infrastructure projects, while gaining technical skills.

Bachelor of Engineering Technology (BET) - Electrical Technology

Develop expertise in electrical systems, including installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems and components.

Bachelor of Engineering Technology (BET) - Electronics Technology

Focus on designing, building, and maintaining electronic systems used in industries such as telecommunications and entertainment.
